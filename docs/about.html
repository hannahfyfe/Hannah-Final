About the Boys

One Direction is a pop boy band formed in London, England in 2010. The group is composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and Zayn Malik. Each boy auditioned for The X-Factor UK, but were eventually put together to form the group now known as, One Direction. The group ended up getting third place in the competition series, but after the show they signed with Simon Cowell's record label Syco Records. Between the years 2011-2015 the band released five albums named, Up All Night, Take Me Home, Midnight Memories, Four, and Made in the A.M. One Direction has won many awards including seven Brit Awards, four MTV Video Music Awards, six Billboard Music Awards, seven American Music Award, and 28 Teen Choice Awards. They have gone on four world tours, and in 2013, they earned an estimated $75 million. As of 2020, the band has sold a total of 50 million records worldwide. If you didn't know about One Direction, you should by now.
